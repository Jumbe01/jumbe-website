About Us

St. Peter Velle Technical Training Center Inc. (SPVTTC) is an assessment center located in Marulas, Valenzuela. It was established on September 21, 2004, to contribute to nation-building, particularly by producing individuals with excellent industrial skills. SPVTTC offers skills training and assessment under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) which equips the students with technical skills. Aside from this, they can also earn a National Certificate (NC) which they can use for employment.
